About the Event

The Global AI Summit 2024 is the premier gathering for researchers, engineers, and visionary leaders shaping the future of artificial intelligence. This year, we delve deep into the convergence of Large Language Models, ethical AI governance, and autonomous systems.

Join over 2,000 delegates for a weekend of groundbreaking keynotes, hands-on workshops, and networking sessions designed to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital age.
